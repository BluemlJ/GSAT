GSAT can assist you with analysis nucleotide sequences stored in AB1 files by performing quality analysis, finding mutations and
collecting information about the sequences.

This program was develop by four students from the TU Darmstadt, namely
Jannis Blueml,
Lovis Heindrich,
Ben Kohr and
Kevin Otto,
in the context of their so-called "Bachelor-Praktikum" (from October 2016 until May 2017).
The code is available on Gitlab (https://gitlab.com/BluemlJ/GSAT).
